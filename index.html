Many Moths!

Photo by @marcusurbenz on Unsplash

Moths are fascinating creatures that play a vital role in ecosystems around the world. With over 160,000 species, These nocturnal insects are not just essential pollinators but also act as a crucial food source for many animals.
Whether fluttering through the night or resting in the daylight, moths are an integral part of the natural world that merit both curiosity and appreciation.
